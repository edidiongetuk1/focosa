About Our Faculty

The Faculty of Computing is dedicated to producing world-class graduates in Computer Science, Information Technology, Cybersecurity, and Software Engineering. Our mission is to drive innovation through quality education and research.

FOCOSA serves as the bridge between students and the faculty, ensuring every student has access to the resources and community they need to excel.
